About Me

I'm Kiana, a 23-year-old tattoo artist known as @meao_ink. I've been tattooing professionally for two years, turning my love for art and gaming into permanent pieces of art.

My work is heavily inspired by illustrative art, traditional Japanese styles, and anime/video game aesthetics.

Outside of tattooing, I'm a huge fan of games like Pokemon, Digimon, and Monster Hunter, which often influence my designs. I also love anime, especially within the Seinen, Shoujo, and Romance genres.

When I'm not tattoing or gaming, you'll usually find me painting with oil and acrylic, exploring new techniques and textures that continue to shape my tattoo style. Music is always playing in the background-mostly K-pops, KR&B, and R&B-which helps set the vibe while I work.

I love collaborating with my clients to create custom tattoos that reflect their unique interests and personalities.

I believe that creativity is a lifelong journey, and I am always eager to learn and grow as an artist.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I look forward to creating beautiful and meaningful tattoos for you!
